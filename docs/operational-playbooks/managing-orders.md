# Managing orders

Preparing customer orders and handing them over at the counter.

## Why it matters
A missed or muddled order loses a customer. The counter screen makes sure nothing
slips through.

## Step by step
1. Open the Counter screen at the start of service.
2. Work orders top to bottom: start preparing, then mark ready when bagged.
3. When the customer collects, mark it collected.
4. If a text fails to send, phone the customer instead — a failed text never blocks the order.

## Watch out for
- Keep the counter screen open during service so new orders appear live.
- Check weight-confirmed items carefully — the price depends on the real weight.
