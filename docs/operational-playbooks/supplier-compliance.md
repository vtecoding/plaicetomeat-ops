# Supplier compliance

Keeping your suppliers' halal certificates current and on file.

## Why it matters
Your halal promise depends on valid certificates. An expired one is a compliance and
trust risk.

## Step by step
1. Record each supplier with their certifying body, certificate number and expiry date.
2. Upload or file the certificate document.
3. Check the compliance screen weekly for anything expiring.
4. Contact the supplier for a renewal at least two weeks before expiry.

## Watch out for
- Don't sell meat from a supplier whose certificate has lapsed until it's renewed.
- Set a reminder — certificates always expire at the worst moment.
