# Handling low stock

Deciding what to re-order before you run out.

## Why it matters
Running out of a popular line sends customers elsewhere. Over-ordering ties up cash
and creates waste.

## Step by step
1. Check the Purchasing screen for "order more" suggestions and days of cover left.
2. Cross-check against any big day coming up (Eid, Christmas, a bank-holiday BBQ).
3. Place the order with your supplier.
4. Book the delivery in via Receiving Stock when it arrives.

## Watch out for
- A product that keeps hitting low stock is selling faster than you buy — raise the order size.
- Don't order more of anything that shows up in your waste list.
