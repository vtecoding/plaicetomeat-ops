## 2. Mission

Give the owner a complete understanding of the day in under 30 seconds — not through a
dashboard, report or analytics, but through a short operational briefing read *before* the
three actions. The briefing provides **context**; the actions provide **execution**. It
answers one question: *what kind of day am I walking into?*

---

## 3. What changed

### The engine (new pure module)
- **`src/lib/owner-brain/briefing.ts`** — `buildMorningBriefing({ doNow, later, morning })`
  → `{ yesterday, today, ignore, wordCount }`. Pure, no I/O, never mutates input. It reads
  only already-trusted V14/V15 signals (the compressed decisions + the operational morning
  signal) and chooses **words** — it never emits a figure.
  - **Section 1 · Yesterday** (context): from `revenueYesterday` / `wasteYesterday` — "Yesterday was steady, with no waste recorded." / "...with a little stock wasted." / "Yesterday was quiet — no sales went through."
  - **Section 2 · Today** (shape): drawn straight from Do Now via `classifyActionType` + `resolveActionTarget` (V15.2), so it can never contradict the actions. Names the most important thing; the remainder is spelled, never a digit.
  - **Section 3 · Ignore** (reassurance): built only from the **absence** of a matching action ("Food safety checks are up to date." only when no certificate work exists). Always present — silence makes owners check needlessly.
- **`types.ts`** — added `MorningSignal` and `MorningBriefing`; `BRIEFING_WORD_LIMIT = 100`.

### The surface
- **`src/app/admin/today/page.tsx`** — new `MorningBriefingPanel` rendered **above** Do Now
  (hierarchy: Briefing → Do Now → Later → everything else). Compact and light by design so
  the actions stay dominant and reachable without scrolling. Fed from
  `snapshot.result.data.intelligence.morning` (the existing V14-truth ops signal); shown
  only when the shop is past setup. Testids: `morning-briefing`, `briefing-yesterday`,
  `briefing-today`, `briefing-ignore`.

### Information firewall
- No metric, number, percentage, confidence, ranking, doctrine or internal value ever
  reaches the briefing text — enforced by unit tests and the live verify gate.

No brain/logic changes: `buildOwnerBrain`, the compression engine, confidence routing,
one-tap targeting and V14 truth are untouched. No new migrations.
